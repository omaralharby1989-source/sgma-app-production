SGMA APP — the official digital platform for members of the Syrian-German Medical Association (SGMA).

A digital platform for SGMA members, combining news, articles, academy content, communication, and member services in one place. The app features a modern Arabic, right-to-left (RTL) interface and is designed mobile-first.

Key features:
• News: the association's latest news and official announcements.
• Articles: medical and professional articles authored by members and published after review.
• Academy: lectures and academic announcements for members.
• Chat: member-to-member communication and a direct channel with the administration team.
• Board of Directors: information about the current board members.
• Volunteer delegations: register for voluntary medical delegations.
• Tasks: follow and participate in tasks assigned to members.
• Profile: manage membership details, membership number, and profile photo.
• Administrative broadcasts: notices and announcements from the administration.

A note on advertising spaces:
The app may include informational spaces for private partners or sponsors within dedicated sections, kept separate from the core member content.

Access to the app requires a membership account approved by the association's administration.
